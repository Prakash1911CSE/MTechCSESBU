Programme Objectives

  • 1

    To impart advanced knowledge in Computer Science & Engineering with strong theoretical and practical foundations

  • 2

    To develop research aptitude and innovation skills for solving complex computing problems

  • 3

    To prepare graduates for higher studies, research careers, and specialized roles in industry

  • 4

    To inculcate professional ethics, communication skills, and lifelong learning attitude

  • 5

    To enable graduates to contribute to technological advancements and societal development

Expected Outcomes

  • Demonstrate mastery in advanced CS concepts, algorithms, and system design principles

  • Conduct independent research, analyze literature, and communicate findings effectively

  • Design and implement complex software systems using contemporary tools and methodologies

  • Apply mathematical foundations and computational thinking to solve real-world problems

  • Exhibit leadership, teamwork, and ethical decision-making in professional settings

  • Pursue doctoral studies or excel in R&D roles across industry and academia

Summary

The M.Tech CSE curriculum has 76 Credits. The structure ensures comprehensive academic rigor, strengthened research orientation through 24-credit dissertation framework, and enhanced technological relevance via Professional Elective Courses including Cloud Computing, Generative AI, Blockchain Technology, and Big Data Analytics.

This curriculum achieves robust industry alignment and prepares graduates for leadership roles in research, academia, and technology enterprises while maintaining equivalence with premier institutions including UPenn's Integrated MTech programme(proposed).
